 HARRY'S

Making social media video content for harrys was one of my favorite jobs to date. I loved diving into the world of social media and working with the algorithm to build a bigger following on instagram and facebook. creating content alongside new menu releases and seasonal events like christmas felt very rewarding. 

 VOSS

Brand video content was my main focus while working for Voss, I was given a brief for each video project, my job was to hit the bread and manage my time effectively. One of the biggest challenges for me was realistic time expectations, i combated this by simplifying my projects and creating detailed schedules to keep me on task.  

 VOLENTARY WORK

To improve my videography skills I created videos in my spare time using whoever would let me video them. I worked with some amiture artists and modles that I met while traveling canada. i would use the footage i took of them to learn different editing skills, i used plug in transitions in premiere pro to create more interesting content. This became useful a few years later when working with paying clients.
