Welcome to my portfolio website

I'm 23 years old, born in the UK, I  grew up in the countryside of Norfolk, a small county in the east of england.

My passion for all things media started in highschool, photography was my first love which quickly transitioned into videography, during college I learnt so much from my tutor and mentor Andy.

After finding a leaflet advertising the IEC visa program that moved people from the UK to Canada, I jumped on a plane at the age of 19 to chase my dreams across the sea. I lived 3 amazing years traveling Canada, every moment of spare time I had my camera in my hand.

I had no idea what I was doing but I was having fun and learning on the way, I practiced shooting and editing videos for anyone and everyone around me, my friends were my models, my colleagues were my actors. I finally got confident enough to start making videos for real clients, I became a freelance videographer and then a freelance social media marketer.

I am ready to start my next chapter in your company, thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio.
