HARRY's

During my time at harrys i had the opportunity to explore design work in more depth, i enjoyed using Adobe Illustrator in conjunction with Adobe Photoshop to create all my work.

One of my favorite parts about the design work I created while at harrys was my client relationship. I would discuss all my work at many different stages of the design and make adjustments in order to get a better result for my customer.

By the end of my time I understood the importance of color layout and order in order to produce a readable and enjoyable piece. I also learnt from customer research that the more condensed the information is the more likely they are to absorb everything on the page.  

VOSS

This is an animated logo design I made for Voss. 

This became part of their social media campaign, and was placed at the beginning of all their video content to create a memorable logo. 

To make this I used adobe after effect and many hours of work, it was a challenge I really enjoyed. 

I really love how design can be paired with video to make a simple stationary logo more fun to look at.  

VOLENTARY WORK

During my time traveling I was so lucky to meet a lot of creative people, I spent a lot of my spare time designing logos for them.  

 My favorite logo I made was 'monkey viper' , a DJ logo that I made holographic by layering blue and pink right next to the main black line. I wanted to make something unique that mimicked the genre of the DJ.

 I find design work to be my most creative outlet, I'm able to think completely outside the box and create content that is unusual and quirky.
